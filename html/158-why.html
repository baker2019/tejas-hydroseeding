Why Hydroseeding is a Great Solution for Erosion Control

Details
Published: 07 December 2018

Tejas Hydroseeding Houston has been providing premium erosion control solutions to municipal, corporate, and residential customers in the Houston, Texas area for more than 20 years. From its inception Tejas Hydroseeding Houston has been at the forefront of erosion control in Houston, Texas. We are the leaders in hydroseeding, applying scientifically tested, state of the art, erosion control specific, hydroseeding formulas to erosion prone areas within the ecologically diverse regions of the Houston, Texas area. Tejas Hydroseeding Houston provides the most cost effective, environmentally friendly way to protect your vital lands from the ravages of erosion due to wind, rain, sun, and pests. Our special blend of seeding is guaranteed to be weed free, and contains just the right mixture of 100% virgin wood fiber and fertilizer. We can even throw in some wildflower seeds upon request, to give a splash of natural color.

Tejas Hydroseeding Houston realizes that for many of our customers time is of the essence when it comes to an effective erosion control solution. Often an erosion control plan is part of a much bigger master plan, put in place due to the disruption of sloped areas during private or governmental construction projects. In these instances erosion control is not only time sensitive it can be workflow sensitive; causing bottlenecks when results are not realized. Hydroseeding delivers results more rapidly than an other seed delivery system, and with substantially less erosion impact on the application area. Hydroseeding's rapid germination rate is often a critical component in an erosion control master plan when anticipating workflows, weather conditions, and environmental impacts weeks or months in advance.

One of most common reasons an erosion control plan is put in place is due to the slope of the terrain. Tejas Hydroseeding Houston has two significant advantages over other erosion control solution providers. First, is our application method, hydroseeding can be applied to slopes where other alternatives are not feasible or cost effective. Secondly, hydroseeding can take root on steep slopes, where mats and hay simply won't stay put. While other erosion control techniques are unsuited to steep terrain like hillsides, bluffs and ditch lines, hydroseeding is well suited to all manner of terrain.

At Tejas Hydroseeding we recognize that each erosion control project is unique. Terrain, soil types, water flow, wind, and other environmental concerns must all be evaluated when developing and implementing a erosion control project. Our years of experience gives us insight into the unique aspects of erosion control in Houston, Texas.

Tejas Hydroseeding Houston is the leading expert in erosion control in Houston, Texas. We get results. Contact us today!
