Many Tejas Hydroseeding customers want to know why hydroseeding in the Houston, Texas area should be their choice in seed application. Although nearly all of us have seen someone spraying some type of green solution onto areas where it looks like grass or ground cover is the goal, not many individuals are familiar with hydorseeding and its many benefits. Hydroseeding is the simple procedure of applying seeds, fertilizer, hydromulch and water in a single liquefied application. A variety of soil enhancing nutrients and soil stabilizing products can be used in the hydroseeding mixture depending on the soil composition of the application area. After the hydroseeding application the wet fiber molds and clings to the soil. The mulch protects the seeds during germination, and then it breaks down over time and provides nutrients. Simply put, hydroseeding is the most environmentally friendly, time efficient, and cost effective method of planting lawn, ground cover, and erosion control seeds.

In Texas hydroseeding is by the far the most effective lawn seed application system available. Generally your lawn is ready to be mowed within 30 days.
