Why Hydroseeding is a Great Solution for Erosion Control

Details
Published: 07 December 2018

Tejas Hydroseeding has been providing premium erosion control solutions to municipal, corporate, and residential customers in the Houston, Texas area for more than 20 years.  From its inception Tejas Hydroseeding Houston has been at the forefront of erosion control in Houston, Texas. We are the leaders in hydroseeding, applying scientifically tested, state of the art, erosion control specific, hydroseeding formulas to erosion prone areas within the ecologically diverse regions of the Houston, Texas area. tejas Hydroseeding Houston provides the most cost effective, environmentally friendly way to protect your vital lands from the ravages of erosion due to wind, rain, sun, and pests. Our special blend of seeding is guaranteed to be weed free, and contains just the right mixture of 100% virgin wood fiber and fertilizer. We can even throw in some wildflower seeds upon request, to give a splash of natural color.
